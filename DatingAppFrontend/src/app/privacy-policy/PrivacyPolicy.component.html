Our Privacy Policy

This privacy policy will explain how our organization uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website/application.

1. What data do we collect?

Our Company stores the following data:

  • Personal identification information (First name, last name, email address, date of birth, phone number and your profile images).
  • Interactions with other users, including likes, dislikes, chat messages, and profile views.

2. How do we collect your data?

You directly provide Our Company with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • Register or login to your account.
  • Voluntarily complete a customer survey or provide feedback on any of our message boards or via email.
  • Use or view our website via your browser's cookies or via an api access token.
  • Upload images or information on your "myprofile" page.
  • Interact with user users through likes, dislikes, and chat messages.

Our Company may also receive your data from the following sources when you authenticate with them:

  • Snapchat's SnapKit API
  • Facebook's Developer API
  • Instagram's Developer API
  • Twitch's Developer API
  • Twitter's Developer API
  • Linkedin's Developer API

3. How will we use your data?

Our Company collects your data so that we can:

  • Authenticate you as a real human
  • Suggest recommendations of other users to you
  • To contact you in regards to any changes to our privacy policy.

In particular:

  • We send your mobile phone number to MessageBird's verification API for the sole purpose of verifying and authenticating you in our application.
  • We will use your mobile phone number and e-mail address to inform you of any updates to our application or terms and conditions provided that you opt-in for this. By default we will not send you any messages to your e-mail or phone number unless you agree for us to do so. You can then opt-out at any time.
  • We will analyse your profile pictures with the Google Vision Cloud API in order to prevent spam or adult content on our platform in addition to providing you with better match recommendations.
  • We will analyse your profile description with a range of our own models.

Our Company will NOT do any of the following actions with your data:

  • Sell your data to other companies, people, or legal entities for financial gain.
  • Intentionally release your e-mail address, phone number, or any other contact details to other application users without your consent.

4. How do we store your data?

Our company stores your data in databases, operated by a third-partyHeroku. By registering on our application you agree for your data to be shared with Heroku for the purpose of storing your data.

Our company will store your data until you delete your account, or until you request for your data to be deleted. If you delete your account, or request for your data to be deleted, we will delete all of your personal identification informantion (Name, email address, phone number, age), and all data about you collected from all third-parties mentioned in section 2 (How do we collect your data?).

5. What are your data protection rights?

Our Company would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access – You have the right to request Our Company for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that Our Company correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Our Company to complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure – You have the right to request that Our Company erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that Our Company restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to Our Company’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability – You have the right to request that Our Company transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: david.fry99@gmail.com
